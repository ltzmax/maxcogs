# WorldCup

FIFA World Cup info and news for your server.

## [p]worldcup

FIFA World Cup commands.<br/>

 - Usage: `[p]worldcup`
 - Aliases: `wc`

### [p]worldcup stadiums

Browse all 16 World Cup 2026 stadiums.<br/>

 - Usage: `[p]worldcup stadiums`

### [p]worldcup news

Latest football news from BBC Sport.<br/>

Please note that some countries do not have access to the BBC news content and may be blocked in some regions, so news may not be available for all users.<br/>

 - Usage: `[p]worldcup news`

### [p]worldcup matches

Browse all World Cup 2026 matches.<br/>

 - Usage: `[p]worldcup matches`

### [p]worldcup standings

Group stage standings.<br/>

 - Usage: `[p]worldcup standings`
